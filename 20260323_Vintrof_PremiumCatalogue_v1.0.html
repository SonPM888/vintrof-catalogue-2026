About Us

Premium Vietnamese Fruits — Crafted for the World

VINTROF CO., LTD is a premier Vietnamese trading company specializing in high-quality soft dried tropical fruits and fruit juice concentrates for the global market.

With 500+ MT/year annual capacity sourced from 5 certified suppliers across Vietnam, we deliver to 9 international markets including Japan, South Korea, EU, USA, and the Middle East.

Our advanced soft drying and freeze-drying technologies preserve 95% of natural nutrients, while our comprehensive certification portfolio (ISO 22000, HACCP, BRC, IFS, FDA, HALAL, GlobalGAP, GMP) guarantees the highest international food safety standards.

Why Vietnam & Why Us

The Tropical Advantage
Located in the heart of Southeast Asia, Vietnam's unique climate and fertile soil produce tropical fruits with exceptional flavor, aroma, and nutritional value. VINTROF brings this natural bounty to the world with uncompromising quality.

Tropical Origin

Vietnam's ideal climate produces premium tropical fruits year-round. 5 certified supplier farms across the Mekong Delta and Central Highlands.

EU-Compliant Quality

Comprehensive pesticide residue testing and strict compliance with EU MRL standards. 8 international certifications including ISO 22000, BRC, IFS.

Advanced Processing

State-of-the-art soft drying and aseptic concentration technologies that lock in natural taste and preserve 95% of nutrients.

Flexible B2B Solutions

Reliable Private Label (OEM) services and scalable bulk packaging. 500+ tonnes/year capacity tailored to buyer requirements across 9 markets.
